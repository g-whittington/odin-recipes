Air Fryer Fried Chicken

Description

Thanks to the convection power of the air fryer, hot air crisps the outside of the chicken, while keeping the interior moist.

Ingredients

  • Skinless Chicken Breast. The ultimate versatile and family-friendly lean protein! As this list of healthy chicken recipes proves, there's about nothing chicken can't do. The key is to keep it juicy, and the air fryer does just that.
  • Whole Wheat Breadcrumbs.For breaded air fryer chicken breast, I prefer to use more finely ground breadcrumbs (vs. larger panko breadcrumbs) because they adhere to the chicken more easily. I also like that using regular breadcrumbs means I can make this air fryer fried chicken without flour, saving myself an ingredient.
  • ParmesanUsing half grated Parmesan and half breadcrumbs ensures the chicken tastes satisfying.
  • Breadcrumb SeasoningPaprika, garlic powder, thyme, and a pinch of cayenne mimic many southern fried chicken recipes.
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oilne perk of air fryer cooking is that very little oil is needed. I use 1 teaspoon of oil per 1/2 pound of chicken.

Steps

  1. Cut and pound the chicken. Drizzle with olive oil then toss.
  2. Add the breading ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Coat each chicken breast in the mixture.
  4. Preheat the air fryer and arrange the chicken in a single layer (you may need to cook two batches depending on the size of your air fryer).
  5. Air fry on the first side for 4 minutes, flip, then continue for 2 to 5 additional minutes (until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 160 to 165 degrees F).
